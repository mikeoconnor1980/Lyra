Change Control & Baselining

Set baselines at any level — portfolio, programme, or project. Lyra automatically detects when changes exceed your thresholds, flags breaches, and routes them through your approval workflow. Every change is tracked in a comprehensive audit log.

  • Set customisable thresholds per project or attribute (dates, status, etc.)
  • Automatic status change to Red and CC flag when threshold breached
  • Full approval workflow — Awaiting, Approved, Rejected, User Override
  • AI-generated risk commentary for flagged items
  • Complete audit log exportable to Excel

Dependency Management

A dedicated workspace to discover, model, and manage task dependencies before publishing to the plan. All decisions are easily reversible — giving you full confidence to experiment with task relationships.

  • Finish-to-Start, Start-to-Start, Finish-to-Finish, and Start-to-Finish types
  • Lead and lag time in days, weeks, or months
  • Dedicated Gantt and Table views for dependencies
  • Save changes in a staging area, then publish to the live plan
  • All dependency changes tracked in the audit log

Executive Reporting

  • Canned report templates for weekly and monthly status
  • Save, share, and schedule recurring reports

Every capability you need

From AI-powered plan generation to time-travel views — Lyra is designed for teams who take programme delivery seriously.

🧠

AI Plan Generation

Upload SOWs, meeting minutes, and contracts. Lyra analyses documents and drafts a complete plan with hierarchy, dates, and status — ready for your review.

⏱

Time Travel

Scrub through your plan's history with a timeline slider. See how scope, dates, and status evolved since day one — ideal for retrospectives and lessons learned.

📊

Kanban View

Tag plan tasks with kanban boards. Status syncs bidirectionally between the plan and the board — giving your team flexibility without losing control.

📅

Calendar View

View tasks on a calendar for date-centric planning. Perfect for milestone reviews, resource planning, and aligning deliverables with key dates.

⚠️

Risk & Issue Register

Automatically generate risk/issue records from Red status items. AI aggregates commentary from task level up to workstream, project, and portfolio — saving hours.

🔔

Notifications

Configurable email alerts for past due tasks, status changes, threshold breaches, and approval requests. Keep the right people informed without the noise.
