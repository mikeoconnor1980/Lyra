Frequently asked questions

Can I switch plans at any time?+
Yes — you can upgrade or downgrade at any time. When upgrading, you get immediate access to new features. When downgrading, your current billing period is honoured and the change takes effect at renewal.
Is there a free trial for Professional?+
Absolutely. The Professional plan comes with a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required to start.
What counts as a "user"?+
A user is anyone who logs in to Lyra to view or edit plans. View-only stakeholders accessing shared reports or exported PDFs do not count as users.
Can I import data from other tools?+
Yes. Lyra supports paste-from-Excel import for tasks and attributes. On the Enterprise plan, API integrations enable direct data exchange with Planview, Clarity, and Microsoft Teams.
Where is my data stored?+
All data is stored securely in Azure data centres. We support EU and UK data residency requirements. Enterprise clients can specify their preferred region.
